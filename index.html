Beginner's Guide to Investing

If you're new to investing, here are some key principles to remember:

  • Start Early: The power of compounding works best over long periods
  • Diversify: Don't put all your eggs in one basket
  • Invest Regularly: Systematic investment plans (SIPs) can help average out market volatility
  • Think Long-term: Avoid making decisions based on short-term market movements
  • Understand Fees: Be aware of all charges associated with your investments
